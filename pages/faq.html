Frequently Asked Questions

How to register for the survey?

To register for the survey, you can go to the "Survey Board" section and click on the survey you wish to participate in. You will be prompted to register before taking the survey. Follow the registration steps to create an account and then proceed with the survey.

How to participate in the survey?

To participate in the survey, log in to your account and go to the "Survey Board" section. Click on the survey you want to take and follow the instructions provided. Complete the survey questions and click on the submit button to participate.

How will I be intimated with the new survey?

You will receive an email notification whenever a new survey becomes available. Make sure you have provided a valid email address during registration to receive these notifications. Additionally, you can check the "Survey Board" section regularly for updates on new surveys.

What if it gives an error after participating in the entire survey and clicked on the submit button at the last for submitting the survey?

If you encounter an error after completing the survey and submitting it, don't worry. Your responses are often saved automatically, and our system records your participation. Please reach out to our support team with the details of the error, and they will assist you further to ensure your participation is accounted for.

Why am I unable to participate in the survey? (Two reasons: 1. Not a registered user, and 2. Technical Problem)

There could be two reasons for being unable to participate in the survey. Firstly, if you are not a registered user, you need to create an account and log in to participate. Secondly, if you encounter any technical issues or errors during the survey, please reach out to our support team for assistance.

Why is my registration request not accepted?

There could be various reasons for your registration request not being accepted. It may be due to incomplete or inaccurate primaryrmation provided during registration. Ensure all the required fields are filled correctly. If you still face issues, please contact our support team for further assistance.

Will there be any benefit if I participated in the survey?

Yes, participating in surveys often comes with various benefits. These may include rewards, points, discounts, or entries into prize draws. Additionally, your valuable feedback helps improve products and services, making your participation valuable for both you and the companies conducting the surveys.

How to participate in the competitions?

To participate in competitions, go to the "Survey Board" section and look for any ongoing competitions. Click on the competition you wish to enter and follow the instructions provided. Some competitions may require additional actions or submissions to enter.

What if there are some arrears in participating in the survey?

If you encounter any issues or delays in participating in a survey, please make sure you have a stable internet connection and try again later. If the issue persists, reach out to our support team for assistance, and they will help resolve any problems preventing your participation.
